Refund Policy

  • More info
  • Refund Policy

If you’re not happy with your eSIM experience, please let us know!

Our eSIMs usually work without a glitch, but if you experience connection issues, we can provide a full or partial refund.

Some conditions do apply so make sure to review our terms below.

These cancellation and return terms apply to all purchases made through the online store travel-esim.net without exception.

Once you buy your eSIM you will receive a confirmation email with the QR code. Once sent, we cannot recover the eSIM nor prevent its use. For this reason, we can only provide refunds in certain situations.

When you can receive a refund:

If your eSIM does not work due to a problem with Travel-eSIM or the connection provider, we can provide a full or partial refund.

Before processing a refund, we ask that you:

  • Contact our support team (support@travel-esim.net) to report the connection incident
  • Provide information of the eSIM, including ICCID number and phone number (if any) and screenshots of the settings on your phone so our team can identify the problem.

Our support team is available 24/7 and we usually respond within an hour.

If the connection problem cannot be solved, Travel-eSIM will reimburse your order.

If the eSIM never connected to the internet, we can offer a full refund. If the eSIM stopped working or only worked temporarily, we can provide a partial refund for the number of days you were without internet connection, discounting the days in which you had connection.
